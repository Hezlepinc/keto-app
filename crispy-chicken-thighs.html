Crispy Chicken Thighs with Sour Cream Sauce

Bone-in skin-on thighs roasted until shatter-crisp. Cold tangy sour cream sauce on the side.

Ingredients

    Bone-in skin-on chicken thighs~5 oz each

    Avocado oil
  • 2 tsp
    Smoked paprika

    Garlic powder
  • 1 tsp
    Onion powder

  • 1.5 tsp
    Kosher salt
  • 1 tsp
    Black pepper
  • 1/2 cup
    Full-fat sour creamsauce base
  • 2 tbsp
    DIY keto mayohomemade — see card. Backup: Primal Kitchen Avocado Mayo.
  • 1 tbsp
    Fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp
    Fresh chives or dill, chopped
  • 1 clove
    Garlic, grated

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place a wire rack on a foil-lined sheet pan.
  2. Pat chicken thighs completely dry on both sides with paper towels — DRY skin is non-negotiable for crispness.
  3. Combine paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, salt, pepper in a small bowl.
  4. Rub thighs all over with avocado oil. Sprinkle seasoning blend evenly on all sides, getting under the skin where possible without tearing it.
  5. Place thighs skin-side up on the wire rack with space between each. Roast 30-35 minutes until skin is deep golden-brown and crackling, internal temp 175°F (thighs benefit from going past 165°F).
  6. While chicken roasts: whisk sour cream, mayo, lemon juice, chives, grated garlic, pinch of salt. Refrigerate until ready.
  7. Rest chicken 5 minutes before serving. Sauce stays cold on the side — don't pour over crispy skin.
Meal Plan Fit
Meal 1 (noon, post-WOD): 2 thighs + sauce + roasted Brussels sprouts in avocado oil = ~850 cal / 6g net carbs. High-fat, high-protein, near-zero carb refuel.
Meal 3 (7pm): 2 thighs + sauce + arugula salad with EVOO. Wide buffer for any hidden carbs earlier in the day.
Why this is clean
Single-ingredient spices (no seasoning blends with anti-caking agents or sugar). Avocado oil for the rub and mayo (NOT soybean-based mayo). Sour cream is one of the few dairy products with minimal carbs and no additives — read labels for "cultured cream" only, no thickeners.
