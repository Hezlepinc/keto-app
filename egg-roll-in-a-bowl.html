Egg Roll in a Bowl

All the flavor of egg rolls, none of the wrapper. 20-minute weeknight default. Cleaned up with coconut aminos.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb
    Ground porkor ground chicken thigh — pork is more traditional
  • 1 (14oz) bag
    Coleslaw mixshredded cabbage + carrot — cabbage is mostly fiber
  • 2 tbsp
    Sesame oiltoasted, dark — for flavor
  • 2 tbsp
    Avocado oilfor cooking the pork
  • 4 cloves
    Garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp
    Fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp
    Coconut aminosreplaces soy sauce — same flavor profile, lower carbs, no soy/wheat
  • 1 tbsp
    Rice vinegar (unseasoned)"seasoned" rice vinegar has added sugar
  • 1/2 tsp
    Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 3
    Green onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp
    Toasted sesame seeds (garnish)
  • to taste
    Salt

Instructions

  1. Heat avocado oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add ground pork. Cook 5-6 minutes, breaking up with a wooden spoon, until browned and cooked through.
  2. Push pork to one side of the pan. Add garlic and ginger to the empty space. Cook 30 seconds until fragrant, then stir into the pork.
  3. Add coleslaw mix. Toss to combine. Cook 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until cabbage starts to wilt but still has crunch. Don't overcook — it should be tender-crisp, not mushy.
  4. Add coconut aminos, rice vinegar, sesame oil, red pepper flakes. Toss thoroughly.
  5. Cook 1-2 more minutes until liquid is mostly absorbed. Taste and adjust salt.
  6. Off heat, fold in most of the green onions.
  7. Serve in bowls topped with remaining green onions, sesame seeds, and an optional drizzle of extra sesame oil. Add Sriracha (check label for sugar) or chili crisp if desired.
Meal Plan Fit
Meal 1 or 3: 1 serving + 1/2 avocado on top = ~470 cal / 9g net carbs. Quick weeknight default — comes together in 20 minutes when you have nothing planned.
Meal prep: Stores great in glass containers for 4 days. Eat cold or reheat. Make ahead on Sunday for 4 lunches.
Clean-keto swaps applied
Soy sauce → coconut aminos. Soy sauce isn't dirty-keto per se (1g carb/tbsp, no sweeteners) but coconut aminos has a sweeter, rounder profile that mimics the slight sugar in traditional egg roll filling without adding sweeteners. Also avoids soy and wheat (most soy sauce is fermented with wheat). Also swapped: vegetable oil → avocado oil for cooking, "seasoned" rice vinegar → unseasoned (the "seasoned" version has sugar).
