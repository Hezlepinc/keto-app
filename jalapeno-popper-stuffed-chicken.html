Jalapeño Popper Stuffed Chicken

Cream cheese, jalapeño, cheddar, wrapped in bacon. The "doesn't taste keto" benchmark.

Ingredients

  • 4 (6oz)
    Chicken breastsboneless, skinless
  • 4 oz
    Cream cheesesoftened, full fat
  • 1 cup
    Shredded cheddarblock cheese, shred fresh
  • 4 medium
    Jalapeñosseeded and finely diced — keep seeds for more heat
  • 8 slices
    BaconPederson's or Applegate — sugar-free, uncured
  • 1 tsp
    Garlic powder, salt, black pepper
  • 1 tbsp
    Avocado oilfor searing — NEVER use canola/vegetable

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. In a bowl, mix cream cheese, cheddar, diced jalapeños, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Butterfly each chicken breast: slice horizontally three-quarters of the way through, open like a book. Pound lightly to even thickness.
  3. Divide cream cheese filling into 4 portions. Spoon onto one side of each butterflied breast and fold the other side over to enclose.
  4. Wrap each stuffed breast with 2 slices of bacon, tucking ends underneath. Secure with toothpicks if needed.
  5. Heat avocado oil in an oven-safe skillet (cast iron ideal) over medium-high heat. Sear bacon-wrapped chicken 2-3 min per side until bacon starts to crisp.
  6. Transfer skillet to oven. Bake 18-22 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  7. Rest 5 minutes before serving. Remove toothpicks.
Meal Plan Fit
Meal 1 (noon, post-WOD): 1 breast + side salad with avocado = ~700 cal / 5g net carbs. Excellent post-WOD protein refuel (55g protein).
Meal 3 (7pm): 1 breast + roasted Brussels sprouts in avocado oil. Hits fat + protein targets with minimal carbs.
Why this is clean
Single-ingredient foods only. Real chicken, real cream cheese, real cheddar (block), sugar-free bacon, real jalapeños. The seared-then-baked method gets the bacon crisp without needing any breading or seed oils.
