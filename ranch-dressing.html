Instructions

  1. Combine everything in a wide-mouth mason jar.
  2. Whisk thoroughly until smooth and uniform — about 60 seconds.
  3. Lid the jar. Shake for 10 seconds to fully integrate.
  4. Refrigerate at least 1 hour (overnight is better) for the dried herbs to bloom and the flavors to meld.
  5. Shake before pouring. Keeps 1-2 weeks in the fridge.
DIY Batch Prep Plan
Make AFTER mayo: you need the DIY mayo as the base. Make a double batch of mayo Sunday, use 1 cup for ranch, keep the rest for other uses.
Uses: dip for chicken wings, drizzle on Buffalo chicken salads, dressing for keto wedge salad with bacon + tomato, dip for raw veggies.
Why this beats Hidden Valley + store ranch
Bottled ranch dressings use soybean oil + sugar + MSG + maltodextrin. Hidden Valley packets are even worse. This DIY version is built on real mayo, real sour cream, real herbs. The dried herb blend lets you skip any "ranch seasoning packet" entirely. The ACV + heavy cream combo replaces buttermilk — gives you the same tang without needing a single-use carton.
