Shrimp Scampi with Zucchini Noodles

Garlic butter shrimp over zoodles. 20 minutes start to finish, two pans.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb
    Large shrimp, peeled and deveinedtail on or off; wild Gulf preferred over farmed Asian
  • 2 medium
    Zucchini, spiralized into noodlesor julienned with a knife
  • 4 tbsp
    Grass-fed butter
  • 2 tbsp
    Extra virgin olive oillow-heat only — don't smoke it
  • 6 cloves
    Garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp
    Red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup
    Dry white wine (optional)or chicken broth — most carbs cook off
  • 2 tbsp
    Fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup
    Fresh parsley, chopped
  • to taste
    Salt, black pepper
  • 2 tbsp
    Grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Salt the spiralized zucchini noodles and place in a colander. Let drain 10 minutes while you prep the rest — this is the key to non-watery zoodles. Squeeze gently with paper towels before cooking.
  2. Pat shrimp completely dry. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil + 2 tbsp butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp in a single layer. Cook 1.5 minutes per side until just pink and curled. Remove to a plate.
  4. In the same skillet, reduce heat to medium. Add remaining 2 tbsp butter, garlic, red pepper flakes. Cook 60 seconds — don't brown the garlic.
  5. Add wine (or broth) and lemon juice. Simmer 1 minute, scraping up any browned bits.
  6. Add zucchini noodles. Toss with tongs for 90 seconds — JUST until heated. Overcooking turns them mushy.
  7. Return shrimp to skillet. Toss with parsley. Off heat, finish with Parmesan if using.
  8. Serve immediately — zoodles release more water if they sit.
Meal Plan Fit
Meal 1 (noon, post-WOD): Full serving + side salad with avocado = ~750 cal / 12g net carbs. Higher protein refuel option for a tough WOD day.
Meal 3 (7pm): Full serving as final meal. 8.5g net carbs fits comfortably under the transition-phase per-meal target.
Why this is clean
Real butter, olive oil, fresh garlic, real shrimp. No "scampi seasoning packets" (most contain MSG + maltodextrin). The wine carbs cook off; if avoiding alcohol, sub chicken broth. Salting the zucchini is non-negotiable — undrained zoodles release water that doubles the carb concentration per bite.
