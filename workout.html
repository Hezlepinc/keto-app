Your Week

Why Squat Day & WOD Day Stay Separate

Heavy squat is a strength/CNS day — keep it heavy and low-rep, no technical oly work when fatigued. Overhead squat, snatch, clean, CrossFit skill go on a WOD day as a fresh skill primer before the metcon. Don't put technical barbell work before a recovery run.

Progression & Deload

Strength: wave the main lift over 3–4 week blocks (5×5 → 4×4 heavier → back-off), then repeat one notch up.
Runs: extend distance or add tempo month over month.
WOD / Bodyweight: swap picks each week — fresh stimulus, fixed structure.
Deload every 4–6 weeks: one lighter week, ~40% less volume. Joint insurance.

Fasted-AM + Keto Notes

All sessions fasted AM. The hard days (heavy squat, WOD): optional 5g BCAA pre-session in early adaptation; scale intensity ~20% while adapting. Electrolytes before every session — same targets as the fast tracker. Rower note: if shoes slip the stirrups, use flat-soled shoes or socks, or sub bike / run / jump rope.
